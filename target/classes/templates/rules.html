Rules

To create your squad, you have to choose 15 players:

2 Goalkeepers

5 Defenders

5 Midfielders

3 Strikers

Scoring

Based on their performance, players will be allocated a point score after each game.

Action Points

Goal scored by a defender 1
Goal scored by a midfielder 1
Goal scored by a forward 2
Yellow Card -1
Red Card -3
Clean Sheet - goalkeeper 5
Clean Sheet - defender 5
Clean Sheet - midfielder 5
Clean Sheet - forward 5
Victory 5
Draw 5
Lost 5
